    Method & limits

    Public posts are pulled from the Reddit API (or a clearly-labelled sample dataset when no credentials are configured), scored with a lexicon-based model (VADER plus a small Marathi word list), and grouped by issue using keyword matching. Scoring is approximate — weak on sarcasm and code-mixed text — and is shown only in aggregate. Not affiliated with any campaign, party, or candidate.
